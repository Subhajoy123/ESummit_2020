About

The Entrepreneurship Summit, organised by the Entrepreneurship Development Cell of NIT Durgapur is usually held every year during the Entrepreneurship Week in the month of February.

E-Summit is the second largest of it's kind in Eastern India. The mission of the summit is to educate and support student entrepreneurs, start-ups and SMEs for creating high-value jobs. This summit is organized not only in the interest of the students of NIT Durgapur but also for all those who are by and large interested in entrepreneurship related activities.

E-Summit sees participation from multiple colleges throughout the country. Through e summit we hope to inspire and prepare the next wave of entrepreneurs who will know how to create and impact and keep on motivating .

Since the very first ESummit organised in 2018, it has seen an exponential growth in its grandeur, we hope to expand its horizon further in the future. Famous personalities like Shaan Suhas Kumar (Miss India Earth 2017), Prajakta Koli (Mostly Sane), Rahul Kaushik (The Melting Words), Arindsm Nag (Anandabazar Patrika), besides other acclaimed personalities have come down for E-Talks of E Summit in the past few years. Various other events organised during ESummit, like Auction Arcadia, Boardroom, Pitchfest are a hit. Apart from this several online events are also organised. The main aim of E-SUMMIT is to offer a platform where colleges from different parts of the country get a chance to interact, enhance and demonstrate their creativity, personality and talents and foster entrepreneurial environment among the youth.

Events

X-CHANGE

Xchange is designed to test your bargaining and jugaad skills to survive with less and using resources efficiently. Is this what you do every day? Then, this event is definitely for you. XChange ditches the concept of money and journey back to the ancient barter system. It comprises of fun games spread over an area for you and your friends, making it a one-of-a-kind. It has to be played in teams of 4. Participation is based on the First-come-first service.

E-Talk

E-Talk serves as a platform for those who wish to learn about Entrepreneurship. And what's better than hearing the journey of those who have carved an undisputed name in their respective fields. E-Talks is, therefore, an amalgamation of some of the finest and most distinguished names from a range of diverse fields. From YouTube superstars to CEOs to Founders to Entrepreneurs, we have it all covered. It's an opportunity to interact face to face with these stalwarts and make yourself noticeable.
